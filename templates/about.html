Our Mission

To democratize access to ISRO's remote sensing universe by combining verified datasets, contextual knowledge graphs, and safety-first generative AI. We help researchers ask deeper questions, iterate faster, and amplify mission impact.

  • Enable rapid hypotheses with dialogue-driven exploration.
  • Boost collaboration between ISRO teams, academia, and citizen innovators.
  • Guard sensitive payload data through layered governance.

Platform Foundations

ISRO-GPT orchestrates curated knowledge pipelines to provide accuracy without compromising classified intelligence.

  • Knowledge Vault: Harmonized MOSDAC archives, mission logs, payload manuals, and outreach datasets.
  • Multimodal Interface: Chat, PDF understanding, and voice inputs with contextual hand-offs.
  • Insight Studio: Export dashboards, citations, and knowledge trails for reproducibility.

Vision 2026

We envision ISRO-GPT as the go-to conversational layer for every MOSDAC data touchpoint— empowering Earth observation cohorts, climate resilience planners, and young explorers to co-create solutions for India's space-faring future.

  • Launch remote sensing learning packs for universities and Atal Tinkering Labs.
  • Expand vernacular support across 12 Indian languages with MOSDAC subject matter experts.
  • Integrate with mission planning tools to streamline payload readiness workflows.

How far we've come

2023 — Conceptualization

Initiated during ideation sprints with MOSDAC scientists to map friction points in accessing remote sensing intelligence.

2024 — Hackathon Launch

Built the first working prototype for MOSDAC Vikas Saptah Hackathon featuring chat, PDF analytics, and voice commands.

2025 — Production Readiness

Scaled infrastructure, onboarded research cohorts, and introduced admin governance dashboards.

2026 — Beyond

Roadmap includes mission simulations, data storytelling templates, and open APIs for partner ecosystems.
