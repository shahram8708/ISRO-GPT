1. Data We Collect

2. How We Use Information

Processing is based on legitimate interest, contractual commitments, and compliance with ISRO directives. Key purposes include:

  • Authenticating users, managing roles (researcher, admin, collaborator), and personalizing workspace content.
  • Enhancing AI relevance and reducing hallucinations through supervised fine-tuning and human-in-the-loop reviews.
  • Correlating usage patterns with MOSDAC mission timelines to prioritize new datasets and product enhancements.
  • Detecting abuse, preventing data exfiltration, and enforcing fair use policies.
  • Providing responsive support via chat, email, or integration with the MOSDAC helpdesk.

3. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We balance usability with privacy. Our cookie usage includes:

Maintain login state, CSRF protection, and safe navigation between dashboard, chat, and admin modules.

Anonymized usage signals processed internally to gauge response speed, feature adoption, and model quality.

Remembering language preferences, accessibility settings, and saved searches across sessions.

4. Third-Party Services

ISRO-GPT integrates with trusted partners to strengthen mission readiness. When data is shared, it follows strict oversight:

  • Government-approved cloud infrastructure providers hosting redundant, geo-fenced environments in India.
  • Language and speech APIs to enhance multilingual conversations (processed through anonymized proxies).
  • Accredited research partners participating in pilot studies under Non-Disclosure Agreements.

Every integration undergoes security review, contractual safeguards, and periodic audits conducted by the ISRO-GPT security council.

5. Data Security & Retention

We adopt a defense-in-depth strategy aligned with CERT-In guidelines and ISRO's data protection standards:

Encryption

TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 at rest, hardware security modules for key rotation.

Access Control

Role-based access, just-in-time approvals, and continuous monitoring of privileged actions.

Retention Hygiene

Automatic purge policies aligned with mission guidelines; user-controlled deletions honored within 48 hours.

Assessments

Quarterly vulnerability scans, annual penetration tests, and security drills with CERT-In participation.

6. Your Rights & Choices

We honor the data rights available under Indian law and emerging digital personal data protection regulations:

  • Access your conversations, documents, and audit logs via the account dashboard.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated profile attributes.
  • Initiate deletion of uploaded files or chat history unless retention is legally required.
  • Opt in/out of research previews, beta experiments, or analytics tracking from the settings panel.

Submit requests at privacy@isrogpt.in and include verification details. We respond within 15 working days.

7. Children's Data

ISRO-GPT is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect minors' personal data. If you believe a child has registered, please notify us for prompt remediation.

8. Updates to This Policy

We review this policy at least twice a year or when significant features launch. Changes will be communicated through in-app banners or email notifications.
